Upcoming

4

Board Vote

Upcoming
Full Board of Supervisors debates and votes. Majority (19 votes) needed to pass.
5

Executive Action

Upcoming
County Executive has 10 days to sign or veto. If vetoed, Board needs 25 votes (2/3) to override.

Checks & Balances

Legislative vs Executive

How legislative bodies check executive power

Budget Control: Legislature approves all spending

Appointment Confirmation: Must approve key appointments

Veto Override: Can override with 2/3 majority

Policy Setting: Creates laws executive must follow

Executive Powers

How executives check legislative power

Veto Authority: Can reject legislation

Budget Proposal: Sets initial budget priorities

Implementation: Controls how laws are executed

Appointments: Selects department leadership

Independent Oversight

Additional checks on power

Legal Review: Attorneys ensure legal compliance

Public Meetings: Open meeting laws ensure transparency

Elections: Regular accountability to voters

Ethics Boards: Monitor conflicts of interest
